About UNO Super Specialty Hospital

Welcome to UNO Super Specialty Hospital, the best urology, neurosurgery, and oncology hospital in Kakinada, dedicated to providing exceptional medical care and compassion to the community of Kakinada and beyond. With a commitment to excellence, innovation, and patient-centered care, we strive to be your trusted partner on your journey to health and wellness.

Know More

Dr. Kalyan Babu Chinnibilli is an Indian board-certified Urologist and laser Urosurgeon based in Kakinada with over 8yrs of experience. He has successfully completed more than 2000 urological procedures which include endourology, laparoscopy, reconstructive urology, and andrology. He has special expertise in RIRS and laser prostatectomy( HOLEP, THUFLEP). Dr. Kalyan Babu and his team performed the first successful ABO INCOMPATIBLE Renal transplant in the Godavari districts. He and his team performed many Kidney transplants with a hundred percent success rate.

Our Mission

At UNO Super Specialty Hospital, our mission is to deliver high-quality, compassionate healthcare services that meet the unique needs of each individual patient. We are dedicated to advancing medical knowledge, embracing innovation, and fostering a culture of continuous improvement to achieve the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Our VISION

Our vision is to be recognized as the premier healthcare provider in Kakinada and the surrounding region, known for our commitment to excellence, integrity, and patient satisfaction. We aim to be a center of excellence in healthcare delivery, education, and research, contributing to the well-being and vitality of the communities we serve.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with empathy, kindness, and respect, recognizing the importance of dignity and human connection in healthcare.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from clinical care and patient safety to customer service and operational efficiency.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our interactions, fostering trust and transparency with our patients, staff, and community.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork, working closely with patients, families, and healthcare professionals to achieve the best possible outcomes.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation and continuous learning, leveraging cutting-edge technology and research to advance medical care and improve patient outcomes.

Our Team

Our team of highly skilled and experienced healthcare professionals is dedicated to providing personalized care and support to every patient. From board-certified physicians and surgeons to nurses, technicians, and support staff, we are committed to delivering excellence in healthcare with compassion and integrity.
