Oncology Services at UNO Superspeciality Clinic

At UNO Superspeciality Clinic, our Oncology Services are at the forefront of comprehensive care for individuals facing cancer diagnoses. Led by a team of distinguished oncologists, our department is committed to diagnosing, treating, and managing a diverse range of oncological conditions with precision and compassion.

Expert Care for Cancer Patients


Our oncology specialists bring extensive experience and expertise in managing various types of cancer, including but not limited to:

  • Breast Cancer Care: Comprehensive and personalized treatment plans for individuals facing breast cancer, incorporating the latest advancements in surgery, chemotherapy, and targeted therapies.

  • Lung Cancer Management: State-of-the-art diagnostics and treatment options for lung cancer patients, with a focus on tailored approaches to enhance treatment efficacy.

  • Colorectal Cancer Services: Advanced interventions for colorectal cancer, including surgery, chemotherapy, and innovative therapies to address the unique needs of each patient.

  • Hematological Malignancies: Specialized care for blood-related cancers such as leukemia, lymphoma, and myeloma, integrating cutting-edge treatments like immunotherapy and stem cell transplantation.

  • Prostate and Genitourinary Cancer: Multidisciplinary care for prostate and genitourinary cancers, emphasizing individualized treatment plans and ongoing support.
